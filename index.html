How To Play

The game is very simple,shoot down as many aliens as you can.

On battlefield you can spot blue rects, they are your little helpers, they throw enemy away

you also can spot health aid and money, pick em up!

last thing, watch out for asteroids



Controls

W - move up

S - move down

A - move left

D - move right

SpaceBar - shoot



Store

in store you have Two sections,1 skins 2 upgrades

you can upgrade your boat with faster engines or blasters

Each upgrade cost 10coins, max 20 blasters max 15 engines
