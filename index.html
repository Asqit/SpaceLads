How To Play

The game is simple, kill as many enemies as you can and collect money.

On battlefield you can spot yellow rects, they are your little helpers, they throw enemy away

you also can spot health aid and money, pick em up!

last thing, watch out for asteroids



Controls

W - move up

S - move down

A - move left

D - move right

SpaceBar - shoot



Store

in store you have Two sections,1st is for skins 2nd is for upgrades

you can upgrade your spaceship with faster engines or blasters

Each upgrade cost 10coins, max 20 blasters max 15 engines
